Geology Wonders of the Narrows

Marlon, understanding the incredible story of the canyon you'll explore will make your journey even more meaningful.

2,640 feet deep

Cathedral of Stone

The Narrows canyon walls reach heights taller than the Empire State Building, creating one of nature's most spectacular cathedrals.

Did You Know?
The canyon removes only about 1mm of rock per year through erosion. At this rate, the Virgin River has been carving this masterpiece for over 2.5 million years!
20-30 feet wide

The Narrowest Passage

In some sections, you can almost touch both canyon walls at once, creating an incredibly intimate hiking experience.

Did You Know?
The narrow sections create unique acoustics - sounds echo and reverberate in ways that make the canyon feel alive. Many hikers describe hearing the canyon "sing" as water flows over rocks.
Hanging Gardens

Life in the Desert

Despite the harsh environment, the canyon walls host lush hanging gardens where water seeps through the rock.

Did You Know?
These hanging gardens are home to unique plant species found nowhere else on Earth. Some plants in the Narrows are descendants of species that lived here over 10,000 years ago!

Age-Appropriate Training Tips

Recovery Priority

At 55, your body needs more recovery time. Include at least 2 rest days per week and listen to your joints.

Strength Focus

Prioritize functional strength over endurance. Focus on legs, core, and balance exercises.

Gradual Progression

Increase distance by no more than 10% per week. Your tendons and ligaments need time to adapt.
